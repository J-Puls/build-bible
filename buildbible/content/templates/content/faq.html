Frequently Asked Questions

What would you like to know?

What is Build Bible?

Simply put, a curated collection of information for most popular project car platforms.

As anyone who has ever owned a project car will tell you, there's nothing more frustrating than not being able to find the information you need, when you need it. Sure, there are dedicated internet forums for nearly every platform imaginable these days, but in many cases, finding what you're looking for on these websites is akin to the proverbial "needle in the haystack". How many fruitless hours have you spent sifting through a decades-old forum thread, only to find the topic leading nowhere and no relevant information being shared?

Yeah, us too.

This is where Build Bible steps in. We collect the information you may need, store it in one massive, searchable database, and present it in a cohesive, logical format so you can spend more time finishing your project and less time digging through the mess!

Where does all this information come from?

The information presented on official Build Bible content pages comes from many separate sources across the internet (as well as from our own personal experiences). In essence, it is the same info you would find hidden in forums and documents spread across the web. We scour the internet for the most useful, accurate information we can find, then compile it into an organized and easy-to-find format, available all in one place!

I can't find the information I'm looking for, I thought you knew everything!?

Unfortunately, we are but a tiny team of enthusiasts, making it impossible for us to know the nuances of every single project and platform out there. It's for this reason that we encourage members of the automotive community to lend a helping hand, allowing us to continually improve the wealth of knowledge contained within our database.

If you feel you possess information on a particular subject that we have not yet covered (for example, details about an obscure engine swap, a tip on getting the most out of a particular upgrade, etc.) or if you notice a discrepancy in one of our posts (hey, we're still only human!), then you're encouraged to make yourself heard! You can contact us directly, we deeply value your feedback! We will then do our best to find or correct the information and get it added to the site for the rest of the community to enjoy!
